About Us

Praveen Kumar L also affectionately called Rider Praveen, is an ex-gym instructor who gave up his job, his means of livelihood, all his and his
family’s assets and savings to start We Save Animals (WSA) Welfare Trust. From paralysed, blind, old-age animals to accident cases, cruelty cases and terminally ill animals
